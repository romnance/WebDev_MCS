FAQ

Can I borrow or rent clothing and equipment when I book a trip?

Equipment: we provide all equipment on all of our trips. Life jackets, ice skates, backpacks, tents, binoculars etc. Check out the tour page to see what exactly is included.

Can I pay on the tour day itself?

No, payment is only accepted by credit card on booking.

What happens if it rains or the weather is bad?

Quite unlikely since Sweden is the second country in Europe with the least rain! Tours will only be canceled when the weather jeopardizes safety. In 95% of the normal bad weather cases the tour conducts anyways. Our guides know many different areas and will accustom the tour so that we avoid bad weather if possible!

What if I want to do a tour outside of the regular tour days?

Please have a look at the tour schedule! Here is an overview of all scheduled trips. If the schedule doesn’t work out with your planning, please contact us and we can see what we can move around!
